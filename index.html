Sector Strength Comparison in the S&P600

This web application downloads the Wikipedia list of S&P600 stocks

and uses Yahoo Finace data to evaluate the balance sheet of each one

The Graph on the right shows Gardiner's Ratio (Invested Capital to

Total Debt Ratio) in each of the 6 GICS Sectors. It is evident that

the Tech Industry has less debt than Industrials and Finance. This

is in line with the difference in returns we have seen between the

tech-heavy Nasdaq 100 Index and the Dow-Jones Industrial Average
