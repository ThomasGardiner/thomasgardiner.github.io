S&P600 Sector Strength Comparison

This web application analyzes the financial health of S&P600 companies.

We retrieve data from Wikipedia to compile a list of S&P600 stocks.

Then, using Yahoo Finance data, we evaluate the balance sheet of each company.

The graph on the right illustrates Gardiner's Ratio (Invested Capital to Total Debt Ratio) across the 6 GICS Sectors.

Notably, the Tech Industry displays lower debt levels compared to Industrials and Finance.

This observation aligns with the differing performance of indices such as the tech-heavy Nasdaq 100 Index and the Dow Jones Industrial Average.
