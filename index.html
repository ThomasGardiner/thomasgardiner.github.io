S&P600 Sector Strength Comparison

This Python-powered web application analyzes the financial health of S&P600 companies. Using HTML scraping, it retreives data from Wikipedia to compile a list of S&P600 stocks. Then, using the Yahoo Finance library, it gathers the balance sheet of each company, and adds the relevant information to a pandas DataFrame. After the data is cleaned and processed, it uses pandas to group the data by GICS Sector, and Matplotlib to plot the results.

The graph below illustrates Gardiner's Ratio (Invested Capital to Total Debt Ratio) across the 6 GICS Sectors. Notably, the Tech Industry displays lower debt levels compared to Industrials and Finance. This observation aligns with the differing performance of indices such as the tech-heavy Nasdaq 100 Index and the Dow Jones Industrial Average.
